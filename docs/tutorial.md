# How to Use
1. Prepare two audio files, such as `Voices-Player` and `Voices-Opponent`
> [!NOTE]
> If two audio files of Voices-Player and Voices-Opponent name are `Voices-{character}` you don't need to rename to Player or Opponent
2. Open FNF Chart Generator Redux
3. Load audio files of Player and Opponent
4. Configure Chart Settings
5. Press button to generate chart
6. After that, press button to save your generated chart

Once the process is complete, check your directory and you will see `{song_name}.json`
